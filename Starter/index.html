Welcome to the magical world of Studio Ghibli

Visionary Director Hayao Miyazaki has been creating beautiful films for decades.

The innovative animation studio never fails to surprise with their fantastical films. Drawing from Miyazaki's childhood, traditional Japanese folklore and various novels these movies force you to reconsider what animation means. These meticulously hand drawn films blow me away each and every time. If you have yet to watch a Studio Ghibli film I would recommend starting with Howl's Moving Castle. It is a good introduction to the Studio's filmography. Below is a list of some of their most famous films.
